Assistant Professor of Computational Pathology

About me

I am an assistant professor of Computational Pathology at the Radboud University Medical Center. My research is at the intersection of machine learning, medical imaging, and oncology. I co-chair the Computational Pathology Group, which develops automated machine learning systems for cancer detection, biomarker discovery and quantification, and improved prognostication.

Differentiation of Prostatitis and Prostate Cancer by Using Diffusion-weighted MR Imaging and MR-guided Biopsy at 3 T

K. Nagel, M. Schouten, T. Hambrock, G. Litjens, C. Hoeks, B. Haken, J. Barentsz, J. Fütterer
January, 2013
PDF DOI

Abstract

MR-guided biopsies were performed in 130 consecutive patients with cancer-suspicious regions (CSRs) on multiparametric MR images obtained at 3 T. In this retrospective study, 88 patients met the inclusion criteria.
